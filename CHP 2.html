The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly has been starting out of her window for about an hour now, On her desk, lying between the copies Nature, New Scientist, and all the other
sientific journals her work had appeared in, was a well thumbed copy On The Road. It has been Molly's favourite book since college, and the longer she
spent in these four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She had spent the last ten years in this room, sitting under the poster with an Oscar Wilde qoute proclaiming that Work is the refuge of who have
nothing better to do. Although many considered her pioneering work, unravelling the secrets Ilama DNA, to be an outstanding achievement, Molly
did thing she had something better to do.
